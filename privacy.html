winwrx privacy policy

Effective Date: 1/1/2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines how winwrx ("we," "us," "our") collects, uses, and protects personal information when you use our app and website. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data in compliance with federal and state laws, including California privacy laws and child protection laws.

By using our app or website, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address.
  • Account Information: Usernames, passwords, and transaction history.
  • Device Information: Information about devices used to access our services, including IP addresses, browser types, and device identifiers.
  • Location Information: Geolocation data.
  • Usage Data: Pages viewed, features used, and time spent on the site.

3. How We Use Your Information

We may use your information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain our services.
  • To communicate with you regarding your account or inquiries.
  • To process payments and transactions.
  • To send promotional offers and third-party marketing materials (with consent).
  • To improve services and personalize your experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.

4. Third-Party Marketing

With your consent, we may share your personal information with third-party advertisers for marketing purposes. You may opt out at any time by contacting us. We do not sell your personal information to third parties without your express consent. However, we may share aggregated or anonymized data that does not identify you personally.

5. Data Protection

We employ a variety of security measures to protect your personal information, including encryption, firewalls, and secure servers. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Children's Privacy

We comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will take steps to delete it.

7. California Privacy Rights

California residents have the right to request certain details about how their information is shared with third parties for direct marketing purposes. To request this information, please contact us at help@winwrx.com.

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents also have the right to:

  • Request that we disclose what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell.
  • Request the deletion of personal information.
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information.

8. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, web beacons, and other tracking technologies to improve your experience on our site and collect information about your browsing activities. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

9. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the updated policy will reflect the new effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
